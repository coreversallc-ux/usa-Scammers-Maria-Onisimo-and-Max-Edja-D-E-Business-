⚠️ Public Awareness Statement: Fraudulent PPP Loan Under Core Versatility LLC

My name is Jimmi Kendrick, and I am the founder and 50% co-owner of Core Versatility LLC. I am issuing this public statement to raise awareness and help others avoid becoming victims of financial fraud, as I have personally experienced a disturbing case of deception by my former business partner, Maria Onesimo.

In 2020, a Paycheck Protection Program (PPP) loan was fraudulently obtained under Core Versatility LLC without my knowledge, consent, or authorization. Despite my name being listed on the company’s Articles of Organization as an equal owner, Maria Onesimo filed for and secured the loan entirely on her own, deliberately excluding me from all correspondence and documentation.

Without my signature or consent, she falsely represented herself as the 100% owner of the company and submitted the fraudulent application with the assistance of Max Edja of D&E Business Consulting (Accountant). Together, they misrepresented the company’s ownership structure and financial details to obtain the federal loan.

The loan—originally processed through Colorado Lending Source Ltd. (now B:Side)—was for $20,325, issued under false pretenses. I have since filed formal complaints with both the Small Business Administration (SBA) and B:Side to report this crime and to prevent further fraudulent activity by these individuals.

Unfortunately, this appears to be part of a pattern of deceitful and manipulative conduct that extends beyond this incident. My purpose in sharing this publicly is to warn others—entrepreneurs, small-business owners, and financial institutions—to be vigilant when engaging in partnerships or financial dealings with Maria Onesimo or D&E Business Consulting.

If you suspect similar fraudulent behavior or notice any inconsistencies in loan filings or business ownership records, please contact your lender, the SBA, or local authorities immediately. Public awareness and collective action are the first steps toward stopping these repeat offenses and holding those responsible accountable for their actions.

Together, we can help protect honest business owners and prevent others from being scammed.
